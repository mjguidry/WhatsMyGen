### Greatest Generation: First tier
#### 1910 to 1915
In 2020, this tier would be those Americans between 105 and 110 years old. As of 2018, the U.S. Census Bureau estimated about 93,927 Americans above the age of 100; the number of people still living in this tier would be some fraction of this.
